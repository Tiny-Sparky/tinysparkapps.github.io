App Description

Short Description

A fast, colorful and modern score counter app for all your games.

Long Description

Score Pop is a simple and intuitive score counter designed for friends, family, and board game lovers. Whether you're playing cards, party games, or tabletop games, Score Pop helps you track points quickly and easily.

With a joyful interface, ultra-fast score editing, unlimited players, and a clean layout, Score Pop makes game nights easier, faster, and more fun.
